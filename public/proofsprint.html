The Five Questions Nobody Answers Before Asking You to Commit
You don't have a bad business.
You have an unproven signal.
Every service business owner carries one of these five doubts before they put money into a marketing system. They are valid doubts. Here is what's actually behind each one.
"I tried ads before. Spent money. Got nothing."
You ran an unstructured test with no positioning, no conversion infrastructure, and no optimisation cycle. You got bad data from a broken test — which tells you nothing about whether demand exists. The Proof Sprint is a controlled demand test, not a boosted post. It's built differently because it's designed to answer a different question.
"I don't know if paid ads even work in my area."
That is exactly what the Sprint is designed to tell you — with real data, not an agency's opinion. 14 days, your local market, a structured campaign. If demand exists, the ads surface it. If it doesn't, you know before you've invested in a full engine. Either answer has value. Most agencies charge you R10,000 a month before answering this question at all.
"Word of mouth has always worked. Why change it?"
Word of mouth is a by-product of trust — not a system. If your best referral source goes quiet for 60 days, your pipeline dies with it. The Sprint doesn't replace word of mouth. It tells you whether there's a scalable, parallel acquisition channel waiting to be activated — one that doesn't depend on who happened to mention you to whom last month.
"I don't have professional photos or a proper brand."
You don't need them for the Sprint. Before/after images from a phone and a clean WhatsApp number are enough to produce proof of demand. The Sprint is built around what you have, not what's missing. We've run campaigns with iPhone photos that out-converted polished brand shoots — because the proof in the image matters more than the production quality of the image.
"I'm not ready to sign a 6-month agency contract."
Then don't. The Proof Sprint is 14 days. One SPOA. No management fee. A R2,500 deposit that comes back to you as credit if you proceed to the Proof Brand. The only commitment is to find out the truth about your market. After 14 days, you decide what to do with what you've learned. No lock-in. No pressure. The data speaks for itself.
"Every service business that scaled on paid acquisition had one thing in common: they tested small before they committed big. The Proof Sprint is that test — done correctly, with R0 management fee, in 14 days."

Kill/Scale Optimisation Cycles
Solves: budget bleeding into dead creatives and missed scaling opportunities on winners
R2,000
Standalone value

Two structured optimisation cycles run during the Sprint. First Optimisation (Days 4–7): kill rules are enforced — CPL over R120 killed, no messages after R300 spend killed, CTR under 1% replaced. Scale rules are executed — cost per message under R40 means budget increase, high DM volume means duplicated ad sets. Acceleration Phase (Days 8–14): winning creatives are doubled down, underperformers are cut aggressively, and 1–2 new creatives are introduced if the data warrants it. Speed over perfection. Data over opinion. Kill fast, scale faster.
